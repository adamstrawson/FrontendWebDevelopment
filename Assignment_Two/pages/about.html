My name is Thomas Hogan and I am a photographer based in Leicester at the moment. I am also a Computer Science student at De Montfort University studying a Front-end Web Development module. I was tasked with creating a website from scratch for this module, so I took this opportunity to design myself my own photography porfolio website. I looked at many different existing photography sites for inspiration, as well as website creation sites such as Squarespace, which are very popular with artists and photographers that want to display their work online.
